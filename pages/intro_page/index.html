Introduction

Hi! I'm Bilige Wuyun, a Data Scientist with a Master’s degree in Statistics and Data Science from KU Leuven. I’m a Mongolian girl who grew up in the vast grasslands of Inner Mongolia, shaped by a rich nomadic culture. My journey brought me to Europe for my studies, and now I’m building my career here.

Professional Background

I am a highly motivated and passionate data enthusiast with a strong interest in solving complex problems and uncovering meaningful insights from data. My professional experience includes building Machine Learning models and developing MLOps pipelines, as well as a solid foundation in statistical methods. I thrive on working with inspiring teams, getting creative with data, leveraging state-of-the-art technologies, and contributing to building innovative products.

Hobbies and Activities

I love going for runs, hitting the gym, painting for fun, traveling to new places, and enjoying nature on hikes. I’ve always wanted to make a difference, so back in 2016 during my university days, I founded a nonprofit organization. Through it, I’ve helped hundreds of students by sharing valuable information to guide them in their university applications.
